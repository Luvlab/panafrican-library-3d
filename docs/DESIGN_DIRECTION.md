# Design Direction & Reference Materials

## Project Vision

"The Panafrican Library Will Not Be Colonized" is a curatorial Reading Room conceived as a living archive of panafrican and diasporic publishing. The space invites visitors to read, listen, and slow down.

## Key Design Elements

### Color Palette
- **Walls:** Warm off-white (#f8f8f5) and brick tones (#f0ebe5)
- **Accents:** Dark blue (#2c3e5c) for window frames
- **Floor:** Warm wood tones (#d4c8b8)
- **Textiles:** Ochre (#c9a227), burgundy (#8b2942), teal (#2d5a5a)
- **Cushions:** Vibrant mix — orange, pink, green, yellow

### Furniture Style
- Low seating (floor cushions, tufted mattresses)
- Wooden bookshelves with natural finish
- Layered rugs creating intimate zones
- Round low coffee tables
- Red book display stands (Afrikadaa signature)

### Window Treatment
- Room 5202: Simple rectangular windows
- Room 5203: Arched windows with dark blue fabric frames
- Natural light emphasized

### Wall Decoration
- Poster wall with colorful Afrikadaa publications
- Magazine covers and book covers displayed
- Dense, collage-like arrangement

## Atmosphere Goals

1. **Welcoming:** Open, accessible space for all visitors
2. **Intimate:** Floor seating creates conversational zones
3. **Cultural:** African and diasporic design influences throughout
4. **Functional:** Books easily accessible for browsing
5. **Restful:** A space to slow down from museum fatigue
